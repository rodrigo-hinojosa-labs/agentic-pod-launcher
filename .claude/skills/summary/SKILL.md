Produce an executive summary of the following:

$ARGUMENTS

Format in neutral Spanish (Chile), Markdown, ready to copy:

## Executive summary
Three to five bullets with the essentials. Most important first. Focused on what needs to be known and what decision or action is expected.

## Key points
The details that support the summary, grouped by theme if there are several.

## Risks or considerations
What could go wrong or what needs watching. Omit if not applicable.

## Recommendation / next steps
What you would do and in what order.

Rules:
- Match the level of jargon to a technical leadership audience.
- Do not add information that is not in the source. If the source is ambiguous, say so.
- No filler, no emojis. Concise and actionable.
